Everything changed in a dark night.

As someone who lives in Turkey while his case all my life has changed after July 15, 2016. Not only my life but my whole family's life has changed. It was as if we were enchanted with a magic stick. What happened? I was fired. I was arrested. Everything I have earned has been taken away from me. Finally, I and my whole family were forced to leave our country.

Was what I experienced after that night good or bad for me and my family? I do not know the answer to this question. But time will tell us the answer to this question.
